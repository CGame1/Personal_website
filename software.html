Selected Internal Tools

CRaBS - Collection of Random Beneficial Softwares

A suite of utilities supporting environmental data workflows

  • FileSync Checker: Verified integrity and completeness of large file transfers.
  • Image Classifier: Ran trained machine learning models to classify benthic habitat imagery.
  • Timestamp Detector: Extracted timestamps from images and video frames using the Tesseract OCR engine.

Various Image Processing Tools

Developed interactive GUIs for image enhancement to support image analysis, including tone mapping curves for manual manipulation, Weibull adjustments and CLAHE contrast enhancement.

AnELiDa - Analysis, Enumeration & Liaison of Data

  • Automated cleaning, validation, analysis and formatting of biological datasets (eDNA, zooplankton, phytoplankton, macrofauna). Integrated with the WoRMS taxonomy API to check and correct species names.

ChemERA - Chemistry and Environmental Results Analyser

Automated processing of chemistry and sediment particle size datasets, generating formatted tabular outputs and visualizations for reporting.

CPIG - Computerized Photoplate Information Generator

Loaded environmental survey images, paired with relevant metadata, and formatted into PDFs for inclusion in technical reports.
