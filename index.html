Research Interests

Machine learning & computer vision for environmental monitoring

Applied ML models and image processing techniques tailored for seabed imagery analysis, with emphasis on generalisability, real-world ecological impact, and domain-inspired pre-processing.

Benthic ecology & marine monitoring

Seabed biodiversity assessment, habitat mapping, and deep-sea ecosystem research using imagery.

Interpretability & accessibility

Explainable ML pipelines and user-friendly tools that make advanced methods accessible to non-experts.

MSCA Fellowship Project

Developing automated multi-modal monitoring strategies of vulnerable marine ecosystems (VMEs)

This project is developing multimodal AI methods that fuse seafloor imagery with contextual environmental data to deliver more accurate, robust, and explainable seabed classifications. In collaboration with the Mareano program (Institute of Marine Research) and the Centre for Deep Sea Research (University of Bergen), the methods are being applied to deep sea surveys from the Norwegian Continental Shelf and the Arctic Mid‚ÄëOcean Ridge.
